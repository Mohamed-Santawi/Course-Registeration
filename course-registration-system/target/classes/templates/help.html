Help & Instructions

Getting Started

  1. Register: Create your account with your student information
  2. Select Program: Choose your academic program during registration
  3. Login: Access your personalized dashboard
  4. Browse Courses: View available courses for your program

Program-Based Course Selection

Important: You can only enroll in courses that belong to your program.

  • Each user belongs to a specific academic program
  • Only courses relevant to your program will be shown
  • This ensures you take courses that count toward your degree
  • Contact your advisor if you need to change programs

Year Management

Year Switching: When you switch to a new academic year, you can still access previous years' courses.

  • View courses from past years for reference
  • Access your enrollment history
  • Review completed courses and grades
  • Plan your academic progression

Schedule Management

Semester Schedule: View and manage your course schedule with automatic conflict detection.

  • View Schedule: See all your enrolled courses in a weekly view
  • Conflict Detection: System warns you about overlapping courses
  • Time Management: Plan your semester effectively
  • Room Information: See classroom locations and times
Warning: The system will prevent you from enrolling in courses with time conflicts.

Tips for Success

  • Plan your courses early to secure your preferred schedule
  • Check for prerequisites before enrolling
  • Review your schedule regularly for any changes
  • Contact your academic advisor for guidance
  • Keep track of your progress toward graduation
